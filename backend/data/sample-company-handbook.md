# BrightPath AI Support and Sales Handbook

## Company snapshot
BrightPath AI is a B2B SaaS platform that helps support and revenue teams automate repetitive customer workflows, summarize conversations, and route urgent issues to the right human owner.

## Product plans
### Starter
- Price: $299 per month
- Seats included: 3
- Workflow runs included: 2,500 per month
- Email support during business hours

### Growth
- Price: $999 per month
- Seats included: 10
- Workflow runs included: 15,000 per month
- Shared Slack support channel
- Salesforce and HubSpot integrations
- Standard onboarding included

### Enterprise
- Custom annual contract
- Unlimited seats
- Custom workflow limits
- Priority support with a named customer success manager
- SSO, audit logs, and advanced admin controls
- Optional EU data residency add-on

## Onboarding and implementation
- Starter customers usually self-serve and go live in 1 to 3 business days.
- Growth customers receive guided onboarding and usually launch in 5 to 10 business days.
- Enterprise onboarding usually takes 2 to 4 weeks because of security review and systems integration.
- Standard onboarding includes workspace setup, one admin training session, and help importing up to 5 knowledge-base sources.

## Integrations
- Native integrations are available for Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, and Intercom.
- The API is available on Growth and Enterprise plans.
- Salesforce sync is one-way on Growth and bi-directional on Enterprise.

## Support policy
- Business hours support is Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time.
- Growth customers receive a first-response target of 4 business hours.
- Enterprise customers receive a first-response target of 1 business hour for high-severity tickets.
- Phone support is only available on Enterprise.

## Usage and overages
- If a customer exceeds the included monthly workflow runs, BrightPath AI does not shut the workspace off immediately.
- Starter overages are billed at $0.12 per extra workflow run.
- Growth overages are billed at $0.08 per extra workflow run.
- Enterprise overages are defined in the signed order form.
- Customers receive automated email alerts at 80 percent, 95 percent, and 100 percent of their included monthly usage.

## Security and compliance
- BrightPath AI encrypts customer data in transit and at rest.
- SSO is available only on Enterprise.
- EU data residency is not included by default. It can be purchased as an add-on for Enterprise customers.
- BrightPath AI does not currently support HIPAA-regulated healthcare workloads.

## Billing and cancellation
- Monthly plans can be canceled with at least 7 days notice before the next renewal date.
- Annual contracts are non-refundable after the first 30 days unless otherwise stated in the order form.
- Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

## Human handoff guidance
- If a customer asks for legal assurances, custom security terms, or a price exception, the conversation should be escalated to a human account executive or customer success manager.
